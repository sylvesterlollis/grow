Square Up: Surviving the Ring & Yourself (Round 5)

Are you ready to step into the ring and fight against domestic violence? Join G.R.O.W. and MatchBout for the fifth round of our exhilarating annual event, “Square Up: Surviving the Ring & Yourself”!

On Saturday, August 26th, we’re bringing together a community of passionate individuals who are determined to make a difference. This event is not just about raising awareness; it’s about taking action and standing up against domestic violence.

By attending Square Up, you’ll be supporting G.R.O.W.’s mission to provide resources, support, and empowerment to survivors of domestic violence, while bringing awareness and education to our community. Together, we can create a world where everyone feels safe, loved, and respected.

Oh, and let’s not forget the powerful speakers, boxing training, live music, special performances, and giveaways!

Don’t miss out on this incredible event! Mark your calendars for Saturday, August 26th at 11:00 AM and join us at MatchBout. Together, let’s fight for a future free from domestic violence. Share this post with your friends and spread the word!
