Bridge The Gap: A Community Conversation

May 27, 2023 @ 12:00 pm - 3:00 pm

Free
  • « Sailing for a Cause
  • Square Up: Surviving the Ring & Yourself (Round 5) »

G.R.O.W. Foundation presents, “Bridge the Gap: A Community Conversation”, part 3 of our panel discussion series where we unveil the hidden truths behind domestic violence and the voices bringing them to light.

As done with our first two panels, we have brought you another dynamic group of panelists offering a diverse and unique look into domestic violence from their professional perspectives and personal experiences. Here, we will discuss the legal system, societal issues, and violence prevention initiatives, as well as bear witness to powerful survivor testimonies.

Join G.R.O.W., along with Wavy TV 10 Anchor & Reporter, Kiahnna Patterson, as we ask the questions often left in the dark, in the hopes of bridging a gap many are afraid to cross.

*Entry Donation: One NEW toiletry item to support our crisis intervention program.
